About Us

The Hustle is a digital Public Relations and business Informative platform which enables the direct communication of the organisation with the target audience. Thus, we have come up with something unique and innovative in the space of digital Public Relations and communication. For the target audience, it’s a platform which provides them the insight into the start-up world.

We at Hustle and Co. have understood that there is a huge communication gap, where there isn’t any single platform which caters to only start-ups with an aim to redefine the traditional PR with the changing world of today

know the founder

Being raised in an eastern tier II city in India, Anshuman was in constant search for opportunities and mostly during his early grown up days he hustled between different opportunities to look for a purpose. Soon after realising the spirit of entrepreneurship within himself, he dropped out the idea of working just for the sake of earning livelihood and took risks to explore the paths towards his entrepreneurship journey. He founded Hustle with absolutely no funds, but just the idea to solve the problem of direct business to customer communication with his idea. He’s a graduate in Finance and International Business from Christ University, Bengaluru and a researcher by nature, he keeps on reading in his free time and has published couple of research papers.

His vision and mission is to create an ecosystem of collective development for the start-ups which he wishes to achieve through Hustle.
